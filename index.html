Our Adaptive Sports Programs

We offer specialized programs designed to meet diverse abilities and interests, with expert guidance every step of the way.

Wheelchair Sports

Basketball, tennis, and rugby designed specifically for wheelchair users with adaptive equipment.

  • Professional coaching
  • Specialized equipment
  • Competitive opportunities

Adventure Outings

Accessible hiking, camping, and nature exploration in carefully selected locations.

  • Accessible trails
  • Outdoor skill development
  • Small group adventures

Aquatic Activities

Swimming, hydrotherapy, and adaptive water sports with specialized support.

  • Heated pools
  • Adaptive equipment
  • Certified instructors
